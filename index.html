A Chrome Extension that saves Money

Amazon Price Alert chrome browser extension is great tool which compares the prices and notifies you once price is changed. You can save a lot of your valuable money only by adding the product to the list and just wait and relax to get notified.

INSTALL IN CHROME

01

Price History Analysis

Price changes over time in Amazon. You can see and analyze the recent changes of price of your selected products

02

Faster Price Comparison

Once your brower is running the Amazon Price Alert compares the price of your selected items periodically. This extension ensures very precise and detail comparisons.

03

Push Notification

Every time the price get reduced you will get notifed with push notification. No chance to miss the deal, just wait and relax.
